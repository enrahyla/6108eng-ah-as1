FAQ's

How can I learn to ski?

All levels of skiers are welcome to join Oval Ski Club. If you are a complete beginner we run lessons in blocks of three weeks. For more information on when we are open and our prices please see our Sessions page!

More Details »

What Do I need to wear?

When coming for a skiing lesson at Oval Ski Club ensure that gloves and long socks are worn. Please also ensure your arms are covered.

Why do you need to know my weight?

We need to know your weight in order for the ski bindings to be set correctly. We do have scales at the club but it would save time if you have this information readily available.

What happens after 3 lessons

You can continue to ski at the club for as long as you want. For instruction you will be moved to one of the intermediate groups.

Where are you?

Information on where we are and ways to contact us can be found on our Contact Us page.

More Information »

Are skis and boots provided?

A full a range of carver skis and boots are available for use. All ski and boot hire are included in the price.

Can I ski with my kids?

We provide family ski sessions about once a month during Sunday evening sessions. All skiers must be able to turn and stop safely to ski during these sessions

Can I race for the ski club?

Anyone can race for the club. Teams are oganised for the summer race league in stoke for all age categories. For more information see our Racing Team page

More Information »

Can I get more involved at the club?

Anyone can get more involved at the club and we are always on the look out for volunteers to help. We are after volunteers to work as ski instructors and also behind the desk for social activities.

Do you offer boarding lessons?

Unfortunatly we are no longer able to provide snowboarding lessons as don't have any qualified instructors. The nearest slope providing snowboarding is in Runcorn Ski Centre.
